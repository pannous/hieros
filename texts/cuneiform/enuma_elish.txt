When on high, Heaven had not yet been named, and below, the earth was not mentioned by name, Nothing existed except Apsû the ancient, its creator and the chaos Tiamat from which everything was created.
The waters were stirred in a single whole
and the pastures were not yet formed nor were the cane fields.
